What we do

We take aerial video of each barrel during a race. That footage is analyzed to show the path and speed of a horse around each barrel.

Benefits

We provide you with a whole new perspective on your barrel pattern. Experts like Charmayne James recommend a turning radius of 4-5 feet (3-4 feet away from barrel). What is your turning radius?

How it works

Cameras are placed high above each barrel to record video. Our software analyzes the video, the camera placement and software allows the rider to focus on the event and Rodeo Analytics will provide the data for improvements.
